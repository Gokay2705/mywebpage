What is our life pourpose?

In numerology, life purpose is determined based on a numerological analysis of information such as a person's date of birth and name. The life purpose is determined by a number derived from the sum of the digits in the person's date of birth and the numerological values of the letters in their name. The number of life purpose is usually between 1 and 9. Each number has its own meaning and reflects one's life journey and life purpose.

  • Number 1 is about leadership, creativity and self-expression.
  • Number 2 is about harmonious relationships, cooperation and partnership.
  • Number 3 is about creativity, communication and social interaction.
  • Number 4 is about diligence, organization and practicality.
  • Number 5 is about freedom, adventure and change.
  • Number 6 is about family, love and responsibility.
  • Number 7 is about spirituality, research and discovery.
  • Number 8 is related to power, success and material matters.
  • Number 9 is about humanitarianism, idealism and universal love.

    • One's life purpose number provides information about one's life journey, abilities and potentials. However, the life purpose number is only a guide and does not offer any certainty or guarantee. One's own choices, decisions and efforts are more influential in deciding what to do with one's life and how to make a mark.
