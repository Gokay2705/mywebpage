What is Numerology?

Every number, every letter in the universe is associated with a planet. It is called Ebced in Turkish-Islamic culture and Numerology in Europe. Each of us has numbers, which are depicted by the fateful effects of our life, according to our names, to our dates of birth. These numbers help us to decipher our own codes about what planet they represent and what planet they represent in our lives.

What Is Your Life Purpose?

In numerology, life purpose is determined based on a numerological analysis of information such as a person's date of birth and name. The life purpose is determined by a number derived from the sum of the digits in the person's date of birth and the numerological values of the letters in their name.
